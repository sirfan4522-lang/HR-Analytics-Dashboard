# 👨‍💼 HR Analytics Dashboard

## Project Objective

To analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, job satisfaction, salary distribution, and departmental performance using Power BI. The dashboard helps HR teams identify attrition drivers and improve workforce retention strategies.

---

## Project Overview

This Power BI HR Analytics Dashboard provides comprehensive insights into employee attrition patterns, workforce demographics, departmental performance, salary distribution, job satisfaction levels, and employee experience. Interactive filters enable users to explore workforce trends across departments and age groups.

---

## Key Features

- Employee Workforce Analysis
- Attrition Rate Monitoring
- Department-wise Attrition Analysis
- Salary Slab Analysis
- Job Satisfaction Analysis
- Age Group Distribution
- Gender-wise Attrition Analysis

## Business Insights

- The organization has an attrition rate of approximately **16–19%**, indicating notable employee turnover.
- Employees in the **26–35 age group** represent the largest portion of the workforce and contribute significantly to attrition.
- Most attrition occurs within the **Administration, Sales, and Operations** departments.
- Employees in the **0–3 LPA and 6–10 LPA salary bands** experienced higher attrition levels.
- Male employees contributed a larger share of attrition compared to female employees.
- Attrition is highest among employees with **lower experience levels**, suggesting onboarding and early-career retention challenges.
- The IT department maintains a relatively balanced workforce with moderate attrition levels.
- Job satisfaction scores reveal specific roles that may require targeted employee engagement initiatives.
